## 🎮 ALL 7 MINI GAMES! (Press G for Games Menu)

### 1. 🥁 **BEAT MATCHER** (The Rhythm Master)
- **How to play:** Press SPACE on the beat
- **Features:**
  - Visual beat pulses on screen
  - Adjustable BPM slider (60-200 BPM)
  - Perfect/Good/Miss tracking
  - Real-time score counter
  - Gets harder as you get better!
  - **Pro tip:** Match the BPM to your favorite song!

### 2. 🎸 **RHYTHM GAME** (Guitar Hero Style)
- **How to play:** Press A, S, D, F when notes reach the bottom
- **Features:**
  - 4 lanes with different colors
  - Notes fall faster as your score increases
  - COMBO MULTIPLIER - Don't miss!
  - Max combo tracking
  - Plays a piano note when you hit
  - **High score** saves between sessions

### 3. 💿 **VINYL SPIN CHALLENGE**
- **How to play:** CLICK THE VINYL as fast as you can!
- **Features:**
  - 30 spins to win in 10 seconds
  - Vinyl spins faster as you click
  - Real-time spin counter
  - Speed demon leaderboard
  - **World record:** How fast can YOU click?

### 4. 🧠 **MELODY MEMORY** (Brain Training)
- **How to play:** Watch the note sequence, then repeat it
- **Features:**
  - 7 notes (do, re, mi, fa, so, la, ti)
  - Gets longer each round
  - Visual and audio cues
  - Test your memory!
  - **Perfect for:** Training your musical ear

### 5. 🐠 **FISH COLLECTOR** (Part of Fish Theme)
- **How to play:** Click swimming fish to collect them
- **Features:**
  - 40+ fish to catch
  - Different fish types = different points
  - Bubbles and underwater fun
  - Relaxing and addictive!

### 6. 🎹 **PIANO PLAYER** (Not really a game, but SO FUN!)
- **How to play:** Click keys or use keyboard
- **Features:**
  - 3 full octaves
  - Record your melodies
  - Play back your creations
  - **Make your own songs!**

### 7. 🎯 **ACHIEVEMENT HUNTER** (Meta Game)
- Unlock achievements across ALL games
- Tracks your progress automatically
- Special celebrations for milestones
